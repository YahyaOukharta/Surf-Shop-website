Few Words About Us

Aloha! Welcome to Funtastic Imsouane! Come enjoy a professional surfing or paddle boarding lesson at the famous Cathedral beach, or other local surf spots right here with us in the bay of Imsouane! We offer private, semiprivate and group lesson(s); perfect for everyone including families with children. All of our licensed instructors are Lifeguard and CPR certified and are bilingual in French and English. We provide a great and safe wave experience for all skill levels at reasonable rates. Come join us!
